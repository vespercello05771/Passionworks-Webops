    Unconventional Career Path?

    Choosing a career path can be tough, especially when its not the norm!

    We're here to help!

    Who are we?

    PassionWorks is a non-profit organization helping Indian high school students find and pursue alternative career paths.

    We aim to solve the lack of awareness, infrastructure, and guidance prevailing in the Indian education system.

    About Us

    Lack of awareness

    7/250

    Majority of Indian students are aware of only 7 career options out of the 250 that India has to offer across 40 domains

    Lack of guidance

    10%

    Only 1 out of every 10 students ever receive career guidance in India

    Lack of infrastructure

    93%

    A staggering 93% of Indian schools do not have career counsellors for guiding the students

    Need of the hour

    1 in 2

    51% of the Indian youth reports that the main barrier to employment is the lack of guidance

    What do we offer?

    Our program is structured into three stages helping the student end-to-end in their career journey.

    With the long-term Mentorship being our crown jewel.
